What is DoorDash

Available in over 4,000 cities in the U.S, Canada and Australia, DoorDash is about connecting people with possibilities: bigger savings accounts, wider nets, stronger communities, and and happier days. We empower local businesses and local drivers(called Dashers) with opportunities to earn, work and live.
